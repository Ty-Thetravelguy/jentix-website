FAQ

Questions, answered.

What is Jentix?
A travel intelligence platform for travel management companies. It captures every booking in one record and turns it into live performance analytics, client-ready reporting, carbon insight, duty of care and a secure client portal.
Who is Jentix for?
Travel management companies and corporate travel agencies that want clear, live numbers on consultant, team and client performance - and a professional self-serve portal for their clients.
What types of bookings does it handle?
Flights, hotels, rail, car hire and transfers - plus the rest a desk books, twelve booking types in all - captured in a single travel record.
How does duty of care work in Jentix?
Jentix monitors official government travel advisories - the UK Foreign Office (FCDO) and the US State Department - plus GDACS disaster alerts, around the clock. Every change is matched against your travellers' actual bookings, so you can see who is in an affected country now and who arrives next, and the right people are alerted. There's a client-portal view too, when you're ready to offer it.
Can we run consultant targets and incentives?
Yes. Build tiered incentive schemes with gates in a guided builder, give every consultant a live My Targets page with their current progress and projected bonus, and close the period with a payout report and a full audit trail.
Can our clients see their own travel data?
Yes. Jentix includes a secure, white-label client portal where your corporate clients self-serve their own travel, trips and reports - with a hard guard that stops your margin, markup and supplier costs from ever appearing.
What reporting is included?
A library of 50+ reports across 13 categories that you can run, filter and export to PDF, CSV or Excel. Share a client-facing report straight to a client's dashboard, or send it as a secure link. Reporting is live on the bookings you make today.
Does Jentix report carbon emissions?
Yes. Jentix puts a carbon (CO2e) figure on every booking - flights, hotels, rail, car and transfers - so your clients can report their travel emissions for ESG, with defensible factors and a confidence level on every figure.
Can we use our own branding?
Yes. Jentix is white-label, so the platform and the client portal carry your own logo and colours.
