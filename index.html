FAQ

Questions, answered.

What is Jentix?
A travel intelligence platform for travel management companies. It captures every flight, hotel, rail, car and transfer booking in one record and turns it into live performance analytics, client-ready reporting, invoicing and a secure client portal.
Who is Jentix for?
Travel management companies and corporate travel agencies that want clear, live numbers on consultant, team and client performance - and a professional self-serve portal for their clients.
What types of bookings does it handle?
Flights, hotels, rail, car hire and transfers, all captured in a single travel record.
Can our clients see their own travel data?
Yes. Jentix includes a secure, white-label client portal where your corporate clients self-serve their own travel, trips and reports - with a hard guard that stops your margin, markup and supplier costs from ever appearing.
What reporting is included?
A library of 30+ reports you can run, filter and export to PDF, CSV or Excel - and you can share a client-facing report straight to a client's dashboard. Reporting is live on the bookings you make today.
Does Jentix report carbon emissions?
Yes. Jentix puts a carbon (CO2e) figure on every booking - flights, hotels, rail, car and transfers - so your clients can report their travel emissions for ESG, with defensible factors and a confidence level on every figure.
Can we use our own branding?
Yes. Jentix is white-label, so the platform and the client portal carry your own logo and colours.
