TMC Analytics & Reporting Features

Everything you care about in a travel management company operation in one place, without digging through spreadsheets.

Consultant Performance Tracking

Track bookings, revenue and margin by consultant, and understand where work is being reworked or picked up by back office teams.

Client Profitability Analysis

See how clients are booking, how profitable they are and where policies may be driving unexpected behaviour or extra work for your team.

Supplier Spend Analytics

Break down performance by airline, hotel, rail and other suppliers across GDS, NDC and online tools so you can support deals and targets.

Booking Quality Metrics

Focus on the bookings that needed fixing by ticketing, operations or accounts, not on harmless consultant self edits.

Operational Workload Reporting

Understand where time is going across teams, and use that to shape training, automation and headcount decisions.

Data Export & Custom Reports

Pull data into your own reports or share focused views with management without exposing the complexity underneath.
