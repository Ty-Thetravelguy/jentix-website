Features

Everything Jentix does for your travel desk.

One platform, built on a single travel record - so performance, suppliers, clients, reporting and invoicing all read from the same source, and your numbers always reconcile.

01

Unified travel data

One unified record

Every flight, hotel, rail, car and transfer booking lives in a single model - attributed to who created and changed it. One truth, not five spreadsheets.

The full circle

Bookings are captured the moment they're made, reportable instantly, sent through to the back office for invoicing, and reconciled to the invoice numbers that return overnight.

Always reconciled

Because every booking ties back to its invoice automatically, the numbers you report and the numbers you bill are the same numbers.

02

Performance & analytics

Consultant & team performance

Bookings, turnover and margin by consultant, team, division or agency - live, and attributed to who did the work.

Margin & yield

See net, sale and margin across consultants, teams, suppliers and time periods, so you always know where the value is.

Supplier analytics

Drill into airlines, rail, car and hotels - volumes, fares versus taxes, content mix and lead time - to back your deals and targets.

03

Client portal, client-safe

Secure self-serve portal

Your corporate clients log in any time to explore their own spend, trips and reports - no more manual report requests.

Client-safe by design

A hard guard strips every margin, markup and supplier cost before anything reaches a client view. Enforced at the data layer, not by trust.

Trips & itineraries

A duty-of-care view of every trip, plus the client-facing itinerary document - all on your brand.

04

Reporting

30+ ready reports

Expenditure by transaction, spend breakdowns, supplier yield, invoice summaries and more - built around how a TMC really works.

Run, filter, export

Set the period and criteria - travellers, routes, references, vendors - run it live, and export to PDF, CSV or Excel.

Share to a client

Save a client-facing report straight onto that company's dashboard, under your own label.

05

Invoicing & accounts

Summary invoicing

Invoice on real billing cycles, per debtor account, with the schedule of bookings behind every number.

Accounts dashboard

See exactly what's ready to draft, confirm and send across your client book, grouped by billing cycle.

Tied to the booking

Every invoice line traces back to the booking it came from, so queries are answered in seconds.

06

Organisation & access

Divisions & teams

Manage divisions, teams and sub-teams under one agency, with the structure your business actually uses.

Role-based access

Granular, role-based permissions mean people see only what they should - consultants their own work, managers their teams, leadership the lot.

Multi-agency & white-label

Built to run as your platform, on your brand - your logo and colours throughout, for you and your clients.

07

Sustainability New

Carbon on every booking

A CO2e figure on every flight, hotel, rail, car and transfer - so nothing is left out of the travel footprint.

Client ESG reporting

Give your corporate clients the travel-emissions numbers they need for their own sustainability and ESG reporting.

Defensible by design

Emissions are calculated with recognised, defensible factors and a clear confidence level on every figure.

Ready when you are
