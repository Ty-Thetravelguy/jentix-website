Travel Analytics Software Features

Everything TMCs need to manage consultant performance, targets, and client reporting in one platform.

Analytics for Travel Management Companies

Tools to track consultant performance, manage incentives, and run your TMC operation with clarity.

Performance & Analytics

Performance Analytics

Track consultant and team performance across all travel product types - flights, accommodation, rail, car hire, and more. See real-time metrics on bookings, turnover, and profitability at individual, team, division, or agency-wide levels.

Margin & Markup Tracking

Full visibility into financial performance with detailed margin analysis. Compare net prices, sale prices, and profit margins across consultants, teams, suppliers, and time periods. Real-time margin warnings alert consultants when they're hitting turnover but falling short on margin requirements.

Supplier Analytics

Understand which suppliers drive your business. Analyse booking volumes, revenue, and margins by supplier to negotiate better rates and identify opportunities.

Targets & Incentives

Targets & Incentive Management

Create tiered bonus schemes with flexible thresholds for turnover and margin. Set up Gate-style targets (Gate 1, Gate 2, Gate 3) with flat bonuses and uncapped volume bonuses on excess performance. Near-miss buffer zones reward consultants who come close, avoiding frustrating cliff-edge scenarios.

"What If" Calculator

Consultants can plan their path to bonus with an interactive calculator. Adjust the mix of flights, accommodation, rail, and car hire to see exactly how many bookings they need. Save multiple scenarios to track different strategies and monitor progress in real-time.

Team & Individual Goals

Assign targets to entire teams or individual consultants. Team managers can set supplementary personal development goals alongside corporate schemes. Track who's on target, who's at risk, and who needs support.

Automated Payout Reports

Generate end-of-period payout reports with a single click. The system calculates each consultant's achievement, applies tier logic, and produces audit-ready reports. Built-in safety caps prevent data entry errors from triggering catastrophic bonus payouts.

Organisation & Access

Multi-Division Support

Manage multiple brands or divisions under one agency umbrella. Each division maintains separate data, suppliers, teams, and target schemes while agency leadership gets complete visibility across all operations.

Team Management

Organise consultants into teams and sub-teams. Team managers can monitor their group's performance, while division admins oversee broader operations. Flexible hierarchy adapts to your agency structure.

Role-Based Access

Granular permissions ensure staff see only what they need. Consultants view their own performance and targets, team managers see their teams, and division admins access division-wide data. Complete audit logging tracks every access.

Data & Integration

Automated Data Import

Seamless integration with your travel management system. CSV imports from Midoco (and other systems) are processed automatically, with intelligent attribution tracking who created and modified each booking.

Flexible Attribution

Track performance by booking creator, modifier, or custom rules. Preserve attributions for key staff while allowing reassignment when needed. Manual payout overrides with full audit trails handle house deals and special exceptions.

Leaver Protection

Post-payout adjustment tracking monitors refunds that occur after bonuses are paid. Automatic alerts flag when leavers have outstanding recovery amounts, protecting your agency from overpayment losses.

Client Portal & Travel Spend Reporting

Give your corporate clients transparency into their travel programme with the Jentix Client Portal.

Travel Spend Dashboard

Give your corporate clients complete transparency into their travel programme. Clients see their total spend, booking volumes, and travel patterns - without accessing your commercial margins.

Cost Centre Reporting

Break down travel spend by department, project, or cost centre. Clients can track budgets and identify savings opportunities across their organisation.

Traveller Insights

Understand who's travelling, where, and how often. Policy compliance reporting helps clients manage their travel programme effectively.

Supplier Spend Analysis

Show clients which airlines, hotels, and car hire companies they're using most. Demonstrate value through preferred supplier compliance and negotiated savings.

Custom Branded Portals

White-label dashboards carry your agency branding. Clients get a professional, seamless experience that reinforces your partnership.

Self-Service Access

Clients log in anytime to view their data. No more manual report requests - everything they need is available on-demand with real-time updates.
