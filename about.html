About Jentix

Built inside the industry, for the people who run it.

Jentix is a travel intelligence platform with one founding belief: a travel management company's own numbers should be its clearest asset - not a monthly argument with a spreadsheet.

Jentix wasn't built by a software company guessing at what a TMC does. It was designed and built inside the day-to-day of business travel - around the Monday-morning questions, the month-end pressure, and the client who wants their numbers now, not at the end of the quarter.

That's why it cares about the unglamorous things: attribution that survives when a consultant changes teams, figures a client can safely see, carbon numbers you can defend, and knowing where your travellers are when the news changes.

And it's why Jentix deliberately stays in its lane. It reads your business and makes it clearer - it never sits between your consultants and a booking. Nothing about your day depends on us, which is exactly how a sharp tool should behave.
